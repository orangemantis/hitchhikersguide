The Hitchhiker's Guide to Coding & the Classroom

Don't Panic!

These resources are compiled as a guide for an adventurous teacher that is setting out to reach for the stars and integrated Javascript coding into their curriculum at large. Most of the material herein is not provided as a turnkey solution. This guide is intended as just that, a collection of good advice to help you find your way.
